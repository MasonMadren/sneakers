  • 3. "Air Jordan 4´s”

    • The Air Jordan 4 is from 1989 and it is not only influential, but it’s also probably the coolest sneaker ever. Not only did it have the greatest feature of the period — a visible air bubble — but it came packed full of things you thought you’d never ever see on a basketball sneaker; plastic reinforced eyelets on plastic wings, an extended heel tab to pull the shoes on and off and an extended tongue that made the sneaker feel like it was more a fashion piece than a hoop shoe
